# United States Historical Public Finance Catalogue
 
A searchable, filterable catalogue of digitised US state and territorial public finance records available through the [HathiTrust Digital Library](https://www.hathitrust.org) and links to the Census Bureau's historical public finance databases.
 .

🔗 Live site: ([[(https://sachadray.github.io/us-pf-catalogue/)](https://sachadray.github.io/us-pf-catalogue/))

## About
 
This catalogue covers financial records published by state and territorial governments between **1792 and 1952**, including reports from auditors, treasurers, tax commissions, boards of equalization, and legislative journals. Each record links directly to its corresponding entry in the HathiTrust Digital Library.

The site also provides links to the Census Bureau's historical public finance series (decennial and annual reports at the state, city, and county level).

### Annual — Financial Statistics of States (1915–1943+)
 
State-level publication (gaps in 1920 and 1932–1936), thereafter titled *State Finances*. Includes assessed valuation, tax rates, per capita property taxes, and state/public property values. No information on taxation systems or laws.
 
| Years | Link |
|-------|------|
| 1915–1939 | [HathiTrust](https://catalog.hathitrust.org/Record/003823986) |
| 1940– | [HathiTrust](https://catalog.hathitrust.org/Record/100216279) |
 
> **Note:** The 1921 report covers only 30 states — 18 states failed to submit census schedules to the Bureau of the Census.
 
### Annual — Financial Statistics of Cities (1899–1963)
 
City-level publication since 1899 (act of Congress 1898). For cities with population over 30,000 (over 100,000 from 1932). Includes assessed valuation, basis of assessment (until 1930), taxes levied, tax rate, and per capita figures.
 
| Years | Link |
|-------|------|
| 1902–1938 | [FRASER / St. Louis Fed](https://fraser.stlouisfed.org/title/164) |
| 1932–1945 | [HathiTrust](https://catalog.hathitrust.org/Record/100122716) |
| 1939–1963 | [HathiTrust](https://catalog.hathitrust.org/Record/002137361) |
 
> From 1907: data on cities with multiple tax rates, special property taxes (corporation, bank stock), and poll taxes (from 1925).

## Features
 
- 🔍 **Live search** — filters as you type across state, document type, and year
- 🗂 **Filter dropdowns** — by state/territory, document type, and annual vs biennial
- 📅 **Year range filter** — narrow to any span within 1792–1952
- ↕ **Sortable columns** — click any column header
- ⬇ **CSV export** — downloads the current filtered view
- 🔗 **Direct links** — each row links to the HathiTrust record
